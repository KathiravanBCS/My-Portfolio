## The Evolving Full-Stack Landscape

Full-stack development is transforming rapidly. Traditional monolithic architectures are giving way to more distributed, cloud-native approaches.

### From Monolith to Microservices

**Traditional Stack (2020-2022)**
- Centralized servers (Node.js, Express)
- Traditional databases (PostgreSQL, MongoDB)
- Single deployment unit

**Modern Stack (2025)**
- Serverless functions (AWS Lambda, Vercel Functions)
- Edge computing (Cloudflare Workers, Vercel Edge)
- Distributed databases (Prisma, Supabase, Firebase)
- API-first architecture

### Emerging Technologies

- **Edge Computing**: 60% reduction in latency
- **Database as a Service**: Eliminate infrastructure management
- **Headless CMS**: Flexible content delivery
- **Real-time Databases**: WebSocket-based synchronization

### Market Demand

- Full-stack developers with cloud expertise: +35% demand
- Average salary increase for cloud-native specialists: 25%
- Companies migrating to serverless: 50% increase year-over-year

### Skills to Acquire

- Docker & Kubernetes containerization
- Cloud platform expertise (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud)
- Infrastructure-as-Code (Terraform, CloudFormation)
- Database optimization and scaling
